A little Bit About Us

Our pizza artisans know the secrets to making a good pizza. Starting from scratch, our pizzas are tossed with only the freshest stone ground flour. Immediately after, our 4 pizza ovens toast our products to the desired temperature each held at 450 degrees Fahrenheit, to ensure that each pizza is crispy and the cheese is crispy. We know that our pizza is the best, we just need you to experience it.

History

Our doors first opened in 1992. This was the yeatr we discovered the skill of making yeast bread, adn the talent of pairing cheeses. We were a local hit, and form that day forward we have been gaining significant renown.

Logistics

Many people don't know, but to keep up with our specific cheeses and local tomatoes, we deal with 3 separate trucking companies. Daily shipments of obscure spices and hearty grains are sent to us which is the key to our success.

Employees

Our staff are put through a week long courtesy course, where they must learn ho to be the best staff possible. We hire exclusively local people, but reform them into 5 star servers, encouraging local employment.
