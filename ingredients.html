Ingredients

In case you didn't know yet, 45 Pizza takes extreme pride in having the best ingredients. We have suppliers from all ovr the world coming to us with their produce, and we happily pay higher prices. The more expensive prices ensure that the tase of our pizza will be as good as ever.

The Flour

Stone ground wheat is the only flour that we allow to go into our bread. The stones ensure that the texture of the bread is tastefully variable, resulting in a pizza unlike any other. The wheat is sustainably grown in the united states, resulting in a truly American pie.

The Cheese

The cheese we use is the best one can purchase. Our pizzas require the sharp taste of cheddar, the smooth taste of mozzarella, and the salty taste of fresh asiago parmesan. These cheeses come together to create a symphony of flavor, all proving the quality we put into our pizzas.

The Tomato

The tomatoes we use are from local farm stands, that we hand select daily. The quality of the tomatoes is vital to our pizzas, because if they are not of the perfect variety, our sauce will taste slightly off. A handful of suppliers allows us to be selective and ensure the best taste.
